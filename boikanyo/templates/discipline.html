Groblersdal Academy 10 commandments

  1. Meet all deadlines, appointments and commitments.
  2. Show respect for others, their possessions and their privacy.
  3. Use acceptable language.
  4. Do not prevent the teacher from teaching or other pupils from learning.
  5. Be neat in your person, possessions and work.
  6. Accept responsibility for yourself.
  7. Follow instructions given to you by those in authority over you.
  8. Behave in an orderly and civilized manner.
  9. Do not litter and do not destroy school property.

2. The Disciplinary Path

A - The 1st and 2nd Detention

  1. 20 Demerits Primary School and 30 demerits High School.
  2. Parents are informed via a detention letter.
  3. Parents must sign the letter and send it back to school.
  4. Learners who do not turn up for detention will be suspended from class for a day.

Detention Classes

  1. Grade 4 - 7: Every Friday from 13:00 - 14:30 (1½ hours) Grade 8 - 11: Every Monday from 13:00 - 16:00 (3 hours)
  2. The educator on duty is responsible for collecting the signed letters, take roll call.
  3. The educator on detention duty completes the checklist.
  4. Absentees go to the discipline co-ordinator for follow up - Monday morning.
  5. The forms and signed letters go back to class teachers.

B - The 3rd Detention - First Disciplinary Suspension

  1. *After 3 detentions the child receives a suspension letter from the school for:
    3 days High School and
    2 days Primary school
    * Parents receive a suspension letter.
    *VERY IMPORTANT: Behaviourial reports must be completed by the class teachers.
  2. *After suspension, the child becomes part of the rehabilitation program and carries a Behaviour Report Book - Primary School.

C - The 6th Detention - 2nd Disciplinary Suspension

  1. *Learner is suspended for 3 days - High School
    *Learner is suspended for 2 days - Primary School
    *Learner receives a warning letter to give to the parents.
    *Parents are called for an interview and sign a final written warning.

D - Disciplinary Hearing (in front of SGB) - 7 Detentions

  1. *Any further detention automatically leads to a SGB Hearing
    *Child and parents must be present.

E - Continuous Offenders

  1. *Continuous offenders may be expelled or removed from school for a certain period of time.
    *Frequent repetition of a certain grade of offence(Grade 1, 2, 3, 4); will automatically be seen as the next level of offence.
